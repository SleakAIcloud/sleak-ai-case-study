ENTERPRISE CASE STUDY

Challenge

A global telecommunications company with 500+ sales representatives faced challenges in consistent training and skill assessment across multiple regions.

  • High onboarding time for new sales reps (3+ months)
  • Inconsistent sales techniques across regions
  • Difficulty measuring training effectiveness
  • Limited personalized coaching resources

Solution

Implementation of Sleak AI's platform customized for telecom sales scenarios, integrating with existing CRM and product databases.

  • Deployed AI buyer personas for telecom-specific scenarios
  • Integrated with Google Cloud AI/ML services for natural language processing
  • Created customized training modules for different product lines
  • Implemented real-time analytics dashboard for managers

Results

Within 6 months of implementation, the company achieved measurable improvements in sales performance and training efficiency.

  • Reduced onboarding time by 58% (from 3 months to 5 weeks)
  • Increased sales conversion rates by 22%
  • Improved customer satisfaction scores by 18%
  • Saved $350K annually in training costs
